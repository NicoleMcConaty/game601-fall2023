My games from GAME 601 - Game Prototyping at American University in Fall 2023

Game Modification: Mystery Block
This Assignment was about fixing a "broken" game. Mystery Block is a variation of Tic Tac Toe played on a 4x4 grid board. Players choose 1 card out of 16 before the game begins, representing a secret block on the board as their own. The objective is to get four of their symbols in a row or column while avoiding the opponent's Mystery Block, which results in a loss of the game.

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Cosmic Enchantments
Cosmic Enchantments is a board game where players aim to be the first to reach the "Cosmic Portal." The game includes special ability cards to advance players through the board and sabotage opponents. Each player will earn these special ability cards by collecting unique currencies on each role.
PLAY
Interactive Fiction: Captain Bjornsson's Lost Treasure
The legend of Captain Bjornsson's treasure is hidden within his sunken ship, the result of his ill-fated expedition to discover the center of the Earth. The vessel is said to have sunk between the divide of the Eurasian and North American tectonic plates. To retrieve the treasure, you will embark on a deep-sea diving trip.
PLAY
Hypertext Fiction: The Evolution Game
In this fictional game world, all life on the planet has been wiped out except for a young creature named Steve. During the asteroid crash, Steve encounters a magical cave, which holds a magical Evolution Portal, allowing Steve to pick up various traits from all the preexisting species to adapt to survive the new climate of his lonely existing world. Playing as Steve, you will have to decide which species traits he will adapt to restore life to a desolate planet!
PLAY

(Final) Digital Game: Garden Gentry
Garden Gentry is a 3D game made in Construct. The game aims to collect all the coins in the garden maze before the minute is up. Key features in this game include my personal pixel art renderings displayed on custom 3D objects, manipulation of a 3D camera and environment, and a custom soundtrack.
